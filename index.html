How To Play

Introduction

This trivia quiz game is a 2-player versus racing game inspired by Mario Kart. The trivia questions consist of general Nintendo questions as well as trivia questions for specific games by Nintendo. Each player will be awarded 1 point for each correct question answered with no penalties for wrong answers. However, beware of your opponent's usage of items!

Using Items

You can get items for every 3 points you score. There are three items you may get by random. The Dash Mushroom is a speed item that lets you get ahead by awarding you 1 point. The Red Shell is an offense item that hones in on your opponent and knocks them of 1 point. Lastly, the Super Star is a speed and defense item that allows you invincibility for one question. Even if you get the question wrong, you will still gain a point! Battle to see who can get more points to win the race!
