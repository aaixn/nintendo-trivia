How To Play

Introduction

This trivia quiz game is a 2-player versus racing game inspired by Mario Kart. The trivia questions consist of general Nintendo questions as well as trivia questions for specific games by Nintendo. Each player will be awarded 1 point for each correct question answered with no penalties for wrong answers. However, beware of your opponent's usage of items!

Using Items

You can get items for every 3 points you score. Click the item box to get a random item. Click the item to use it! Don't worry about missing an opportunity to use your item, you may use your item at any point, even if it is not your turn!

There are three items you may get by random. The Dash Mushroom is a speed item that lets you get ahead by awarding you 1 point. The Red Shell is an offense item that hones in on your opponent and knocks off 1 point. Lastly, the Super Star is a super speed item that gives you a super boost awarding you 2 points! Battle to see who can get more points to win the race!
